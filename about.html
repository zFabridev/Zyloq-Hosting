Our Journey

Company Timeline

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted hosting provider.

2020

Foundation

Zyloq Hosting was founded by a group of gamers and developers frustrated with the poor performance of existing hosting solutions. We started with a single server in a New York data center.

2021

First Major Upgrade

Upgraded to AMD Ryzen 9 processors and implemented our custom control panel. Reached 1,000 customers milestone.

2022

Global Expansion

Expanded to European and Asian data centers. Launched VPS and Web Hosting services. Implemented enterprise DDoS protection.

2023

Ryzen 9 9950X Launch

Became one of the first hosting providers to deploy AMD Ryzen 9 9950X servers. Reached 5,000 customers across 50+ countries.
